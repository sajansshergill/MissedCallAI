## PRO TIPS

1. **Practice the call** - Do it a few times before the demo
2. **Be natural** - Don't sound scripted
3. **Pause for questions** - Let them ask as you go
4. **Show enthusiasm** - You believe in this, show it
5. **Handle objections** - Be ready for "what if" questions
6. **Close strong** - Always end with a clear next step
