## POST-CALL FOLLOW-UP

### If They Say Yes:
1. Send onboarding email immediately
2. Schedule setup call
3. Send contract/invoice
4. Set up the system
5. Do a test call together
6. Check in after 3 days

### If They Say "Let me think about it":
1. Send follow-up email with:
   - ROI calculator
   - Case study (if you have one)
   - Demo video link
2. Follow up in 3 days
3. Offer limited-time discount (optional)

### If They Say No:
1. Ask why (get feedback)
2. Leave the door open: "If anything changes, let me know"
3. Add to nurture sequence
