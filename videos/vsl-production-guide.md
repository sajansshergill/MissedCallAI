## üé¨ Production Steps

### Step 1: Prepare Your Demo (15 minutes)

1. **Set up your Twilio number**
   - Make sure it's working
   - Test the AI agent
   - Have a test conversation ready

2. **Prepare your screen**
   - Close unnecessary apps
   - Open Google Sheets (to show lead entry)
   - Have your phone ready (to show SMS)
   - Clear browser tabs

3. **Test the flow**
   - Make a test call
   - Verify AI answers
   - Check SMS is sent
   - Verify Google Sheet entry

### Step 2: Record the Video (30 minutes)

**Using Loom (Recommended):**

1. **Go to loom.com** and sign up (free)
2. **Click "New Recording"**
3. **Choose:**
   - Screen + Camera (optional)
   - Full screen or window
   - Microphone enabled

4. **Follow the script** from `vsl-script.md`:
   - Start recording
   - Follow the script section by section
   - Don't worry about mistakes - you can re-record sections

5. **Key moments to capture:**
   - Problem explanation (screen share)
   - Live call demo (screen share + audio)
   - Google Sheets showing lead (screen share)
   - SMS confirmation (phone screen or screen share)

### Step 3: Edit (Optional, 20 minutes)

**Basic edits:**
- Cut out long pauses
- Remove "umms" and "ahhs"
- Add text overlays for key points
- Add captions (Loom does this automatically)

**Advanced edits (if using ScreenFlow/Camtasia):**
- Add transitions
- Add background music (subtle)
- Add callouts/arrows
- Add logo/branding

## üìù Recording Tips

### Voice:
- Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- Show enthusiasm (but not hypey)
- Pause between key points
- Smile while talking (it shows in your voice!)

### Screen Recording:
- Use full screen for demos
- Zoom in on important parts (phone, Google Sheet)
- Move mouse cursor slowly
- Highlight important elements

### Timing:
- Keep it under 3 minutes
- Problem: 30 seconds
- Demo: 90 seconds
- Results: 30 seconds
- CTA: 30 seconds

## üé¨ Recording Script Breakdown

### Section 1: Hook (0:00 - 0:15)
**Visual:** Your face or screen with text overlay
**Action:** Speak directly to camera
**Script:** "Here's what happens when you miss a call..."

### Section 2: Problem (0:15 - 0:45)
**Visual:** Screen share showing statistics or examples
**Action:** Explain the problem
**Script:** "Most home service businesses miss 30-40% of calls..."

### Section 3: Demo (0:45 - 2:15)
**Visual:** Screen recording of live call
**Action:** Make actual call, show conversation
**Script:** "Let me show you what happens with MissedCallAI..."

### Section 4: Results (2:15 - 2:45)
**Visual:** Screen share of Google Sheet, SMS
**Action:** Show the captured lead
**Script:** "Look at this. The lead is already in the system..."

### Section 5: CTA (2:45 - 3:00)
**Visual:** Website or booking link
**Action:** Direct call-to-action
**Script:** "If you're ready to stop missing calls..."
