## üé® Design Tips

### Colors:
- Primary: Blue (#2563eb) - Trust, professional
- Secondary: Green (#10b981) - Success, money
- Accent: Orange/Red - Urgency, problem
- Background: White or dark gray

### Fonts:
- Headers: Bold, sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, Arial)
- Body: Clean, readable (same family)
- Size: Large enough to read on mobile

### Layout:
- Keep it simple
- One main point per slide
- Lots of white space
- Use icons, not clipart

### Animation:
- Simple fade-ins
- Slide transitions
- Don't overdo it
- Keep it professional
