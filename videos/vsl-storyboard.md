## ðŸŽ¬ Production Notes

### Transitions:
- Smooth fades between scenes
- No jarring cuts
- Professional feel

### Text Overlays:
- Large, readable font
- High contrast
- Don't block important visuals
- Appear/disappear smoothly

### Audio:
- Clear narration
- No background noise
- Consistent volume
- Enthusiastic but not hypey

### Timing:
- Keep scenes moving
- Don't linger too long
- Maintain energy
- Total: 3 minutes 15 seconds
